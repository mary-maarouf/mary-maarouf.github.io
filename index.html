Sushi

Sushi is a popular Japanese food. In Japanese cuisine, sushi is made from pickled rice and covered with skinned, skinless, thin strips of raw fish, or the rice is wrapped around various fillings of seafood, vegetables, breakfast, fish eggs, or red meat.
